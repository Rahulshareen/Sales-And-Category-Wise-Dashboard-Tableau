🔍 Key Findings

Technology leads all categories at $836K — outperforming Furniture ($742K) and Office Supplies ($719K)
West region dominates with 31.58% of sales, followed by East (29.55%) — together accounting for 61% of total revenue
California alone contributes $457K — more than entire regions combined; Washington is second at $138K
Peak sales month is November 2017 at $118K — clear seasonal spike suggesting holiday-driven demand
Top 3 customers (Sean Miller, Tamara Chand, Raymond Buch) contribute $53,781 — concentrated high-value segment worth protecting

💡 Business Recommendations

Prioritise Technology inventory — highest revenue category with likely better margins than Furniture
Replicate West region strategy in underperforming Central (17.05%) and South regions
Build a retention programme around top 10 customers — small group, disproportionate revenue impact
Align stock and campaigns with November peak — data confirms holiday seasonality
